## Pros and cons

The main strengths are Zigbee support, broad listed ecosystem coverage, app control, and the lowest catalog price among the three compared hubs. Apple HomeKit support is a meaningful differentiator against Aeotec's profile, while Alexa and Google Home support keep the hub relevant for common voice ecosystems.

The central tradeoff is the absence of Matter in the profile. That makes Aqara a less direct fit for a buyer whose selection criteria start with Matter. The hub also should not be chosen for energy monitoring, camera features, audio features, or lighting-control fields because the profile does not list those capabilities.
